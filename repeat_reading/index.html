📖 How to Use Reading Tracker

1
Add Your Content
Enter a title and URL for any website, article, tutorial, or video you want to track. This could be documentation, blog posts, online courses, or any educational content.
2
Track Your Reads
Click "+1 Read" every time you visit or read the content. Watch as your cards change color to show your progress - from white to darker shades as you read more.
3
Monitor Progress
View your reading statistics at the top and see which content you've engaged with most. Use the "Copy" button to easily share links with others.
4
Manage Your List
Use "-1" to decrease counts if needed, delete items you no longer need, or clear all data to start fresh. All data is stored locally in your browser.
